About

Bente Wesselink is a Dutch model based in Amsterdam, represented by De Boekers. With a distinctive presence and versatile range, she has worked with acclaimed photographers, leading fashion houses, and global brands across editorial, commercial, and runway projects.

Known for her effortless elegance and natural ability in front of the camera, Bente brings both professionalism and creativity to every project. Whether on set or on the runway, she brings a quiet intensity that translates beautifully across every medium.
